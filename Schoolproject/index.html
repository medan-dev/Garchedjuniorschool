About Us

At Garch Ed Junior School, we believe in providing a holistic education that nurtures the mind, body, and spirit of every child. Our dedicated staff is committed to fostering a safe and stimulating environment where children can thrive academically and socially.

Our Programs

  • Early Childhood Education: A play-based curriculum designed for young learners to explore and discover.
  • STEM Programs: Engaging activities in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics to inspire future innovators.
  • Arts and Culture: Encouraging creativity through visual arts, music, and performing arts.
  • Physical Education: Promoting health and fitness through various sports and activities.
